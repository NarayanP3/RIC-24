Greetings from Team Research and Industrial Conclave IIT Guwahati

 

It gives us immense pleasure to announce the most significant and exciting event of the year "Research & Industrial Conclave-Integration'23", the eight consecutive meeting, one of its kind, jointly organized by the Students’ Academic Board (SAB), IIT Guwahati and IIT Guwahati ResearchPark which is going to be held during May 14 - 16, 2023.  The event “Research and Industrial Conclave-Integration’23” stands as an efficacious platform for motivating young minds towards research, innovation, and entrepreneurship. It intends to promote a shared vision of understanding of scientific endeavor and curiosity. 

 

These are the highlights of RIC 2023: 

  1. Scientifique - Oral, poster, model, and three-minute thesis presentation to showcase your original research work 
  2. Atelier - Workshops with immersive learning and discussions 
  3. Connaissance - Lecture series featuring leading academicians and industry experts 
  4. Discutir - Panel discussions on the most relevant scientific topics of our times 
  5. Emprendimiento - Platform for start-ups to present their concept, network and collaborate, and to raise funds to launch or upscale their enterprise 
  6. Hackathon - Formulation of holistic solutions for pressing social problems through technological intervention, and the joint engagement of governance and industry to foster start-ups that implement these solutions 
  7. Indsol - Multi-stakeholder (industries, organizations, start-ups, entrepreneurs) solution presentation with added opportunity for long term collaboration with students, scientists and researchers interested in the field 
  8. Tech Talk - Platform for scholars and industry experts to present research output to government and industry representatives, with rigorous jury evaluation based on futuristic application and commercial viability 
  9. Innovation Exhibit - Showcasing products, prototypes and services designed by corporates, PSUs, These are the highlights of RIC 2023 MSMEs, start-ups, entrepreneurs, and students so as to commercialize these creations
