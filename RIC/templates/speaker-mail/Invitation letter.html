As IIT Guwahati is celebrating 25 years of excellence, it gives us immense pleasure to announce
Research Conclave20, during March 19 - 22, 2020, the sixth consecutive meeting of the sort
organized by the Students Academic Board (SAB), IIT Guwahati (IITG).
The event provides a unique platform to nurture young minds towards research, innovation, and
entrepreneurship. It promises a wonderful opportunity for the budding researchers to interact with
eminent personalities from academic and industrial research. Furthermore, Research Conclave is
designed to catalyze cross-disciplinary interactions, as well as to promote industry-academia
collaborations. Initiated in 2015 with the above objectives, this conclave has evolved into one of
the most popular meetings of its kind in the country, organized by and for the students.
The meeting includes lectures by eminent personalities from industry and academia, panel
discussions, scientific presentations by the participants (oral, poster or model), startup challenge,
technical exhibitions, workshops, etc. Additionally, activities such as quizzes, debates and
laboratory visits have also been planned so as to provide an exposure to various challenges before
humanity, be it related to renewable energy, environment conservation, cyber security, robotics,
mass education, innovative designs, and to the evolving state of the art scientific innovations to
this cause. Essentially, the current day academic research being evermore super-specialized, the
young researchers find it hard to realize the scope/utility of their work to the outstanding concerns
of humanity. In a nutshell, this is where this meeting has its focus.
Therefore, we are requesting active participation of the students of your prestigious institution.
Please encourage and mobilize research scholars, also postgraduates and undergraduates interested
in a research career, to attend the meeting. The modalities of participation are enclosed herewith
with the leaflet.
Looking forward to an enthusiastic participation from your institute in the Research Conclave 20
at IIT Guwahati.
Thank you!
