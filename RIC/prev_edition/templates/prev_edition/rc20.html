About Research Conclave 2020

Research Conclave is an event organized by the Studentsâ€™ Academic Board (SAB) of Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG). Started in 2015, Research Conclave has become a veritable platform to nurture the young minds towards research, innovation and entrepreneurship and intends to bring the undivided attention of the students towards both industries and academia to redress the academic research challenges, concerns of the entire student community and upcoming entrepreneurs around the globe. It is a forum to harness innovative minds of current society by taking innovations made in research to their actual implementation in the industries. Research Conclave aims to be the catalyst which builds leaders through holistic, transformable and innovative ideas.
Research Conclave has gone from strength to strength since its inception and now boasts of being one of the largest of its kind in India with the 2019 edition witnessing participants from over 30 states and union territories of our country.








Objective of Research Conclave 2020

  • Showcasing the ongoing research activities by research groups of different educational institutions across the country.
  • Providing a platform for exchange of thoughts between distinguished guests and participants from different cultural and scientific backgrounds.
  • Facilitating industry - academia interaction with emphasis on the technological requirements and expectations of and from research institutes.
  • Inspiring and encouraging the undergraduate student community to pursue a career in research.
  • Acting as a platform for budding startups to showcase their idea and to provide them with necessary guidance through established entrepreneurs and firms.
